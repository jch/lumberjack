Lumberjack Logger

- the smarter way to view your Rails log files on the Mac.

As a Rails developer, it's likely that you spend many hours staring at your development log scroll by in a terminal. For something you spend so much time with, isn't it time to rethink what's possible?

Clearly Formatted

Logger separates each request into it's own easy-to-read timestamped section. It's easy to highlight a section so you don't lose your place. Logger also hides common log noise, but still lets you see them when you need it.

Sharing

When you get stuck on a problem, it's tricky to highlight what you need to ask your team for help. With Logger's [gist]() support, it's a snap to copy and show your team the problem.

Search and Highlight

No more squinting to look for that line mixed in with all that SQL gobbledgook. Use the same beautiful and intuitive search Cocoa gives you.

Open in Editor

Hit an exception? Just click on the line, and have it open in your preferred editor.
